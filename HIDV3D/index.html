Hand Integrated Data Visualisation 3D — explore your data in three dimensions, controlled by your mouse or hand gestures.

Upload a CSV

Click Upload CSV to load any comma separated data file. The system automatically detects columns and suggests the best axes.

Mouse Controls

Click and drag to rotate. Scroll to zoom. Right click and drag to pan.

✋
Hand Gesture Controls

Click Hand Control to activate your webcam. Open palm rotates the scene, closed fist zooms out, fingers together zooms in.
